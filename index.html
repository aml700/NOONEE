NOONEE DIAMOND

Nooni Jewelry uses only diamonds that have not undergone illegal processing.
We only use stones supplied by reliable sightholders and issued an appraisal from GIA.
This is to ensure that the finest diamonds are used in precious jewellery.

The 4Cs, created by GIA, are now commonly used as standard for evaluating diamonds.
In other words, GIA's appraisal standards and appraisal are the clearest and most reliable.

The 4Cs of GIA consist of Color, Clarity, Cut, and Carat.
